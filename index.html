What is this?

This website is a way for me to host things that I feel will be useful to have accessible over the internet. It started out as a way to organize and distribute information on a TTRPG ruleset I am creating, but who knows what you might be able to find here in the future.

Note

This whole site is still very much a work in progress. Suggestions, ideas and criticism are welcome.
